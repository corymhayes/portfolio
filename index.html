Guitar Tab Favorites

http://github.com/corymhayes

I began teaching myself to play guitar many years ago and one of the things that helped me to learn were tabs. As such over the years I have developed a certain catalogue of songs that I like playing and typically always end up right back on that same tab. So I figured why not develop a simple database and a table to display all my favorite tabs. Now not only are my favorite tabs in one place, its also sortable, so if I want to keep in a certain tuning or just feel playing songs by a certain artist, it's all one click away.

Tools used: Adobe XD, React.js, Firebase, git
